About Custom Mortgage and Loans

Your Personal Mortgage and Loan Broker Service Sunshine Coast Qld.

We feel that it's of prime importance to be fully informed of ALL loan options available, for whatever purpose, whether that’s securing your first or third home, vehicle, business loan or refinance. We find satisfaction from seeing people hit their milestones. Even just helping clients save money by refinancing their existing loans makes the job worth it in itself.

We strive to be informative, sharing our wealth of information to expand your knowledge of the loan process. We also give you the hard facts you need to make these important financial decisions. And along the way you are handheld from first consultation right through to settlement.

Ultimately, Custom Mortgage and Loans’ mission is to guide you into the most suitable match of loan and lender that serves your need and help you move closer to your life’s goals.

Custom Mortgage and Loans set themselves apart by establishing trust early on, making it clear that we have your best interests at heart. You will get a sense of this from the first consultation - we will meet with you and we will listen to your needs and together with you find the most suitable strategy to assist you in reaching your goals. We will work with your service providers such as real estate agent, conveyancer and lender during the loan process giving you more peace of mind. We are your single point of contact.

Custom Mortgage and Loans are a mobile broker. As such, we are available all days/nights and times (within reason!). Contact us by phone or email for a complimentary review of your current finances today.

Custom Mortgage and Loans is happy to assist in a variety of services such as: Home loans, refinance, construction loans, land purchase, bridging loans, vehicle finance, asset finance, reverse mortgages, business and home insurance to name a few.
